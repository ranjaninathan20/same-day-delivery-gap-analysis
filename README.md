# Same-Day Delivery Gap Analysis — Myntra & Ajio vs Quick Commerce
Competitive analysis of same-day fashion delivery vs quick commerce in India

---
## Project Snapshot

**Goal**  
Evaluate how competitive same-day fashion delivery is in India by comparing traditional fashion platforms with quick commerce players.

**Dataset**  
Competitive analysis of 13 platforms across delivery speed, city coverage, minimum order value, delivery fee, and market share.

**Tools**  
Excel · Process Analysis (draw.io) · Business Requirements Documentation

**Key Insight**  
Fashion platforms operate same-day delivery in very limited cities and require much higher minimum order values compared to quick commerce, creating a significant adoption gap.


## Project Overview

This project compares **13 e-commerce and quick commerce platforms** to understand 
how competitive **same-day fashion delivery** is in India and what factors are 
slowing its adoption.

The goal is to identify the **operational and pricing gaps between traditional 
fashion platforms and quick commerce platforms**.

---

## Business Questions

This analysis explores the following questions:

- How does **city coverage** compare between fashion e-commerce and quick commerce?
- Are **minimum order values and delivery fees** preventing same-day fashion adoption?
- How large is the **market share gap** between the two models?
- What operational changes would allow fashion platforms to scale same-day delivery?

---

## Dataset

The dataset compares **13 platforms** across **five key metrics**:

- Delivery speed
- City coverage
- Minimum order value
- Delivery fee
- Market share

Platforms analysed include both **fashion e-commerce platforms** and 
**quick commerce players** operating in India.

## Key Insights

- **Myntra M-Now operates in only 4 metro cities**, while Blinkit covers **30+ cities**
- **Minimum order value for Myntra same-day delivery is ₹799**, compared to **₹99 on Blinkit**
- Same-day fashion delivery currently holds **~3% market share**, indicating a major growth gap

These findings suggest that **pricing strategy, logistics scale, and city coverage 
are the primary barriers to scaling same-day fashion delivery.**
